Instructions

Thank you for your interest in ASA! Please see the timeline below and block out one of the interview dates in your calendar in case you are invited to interview. Once decisions regarding interviews have been made, you will be contacted by the Executive Board.

All applicants are expected to respond to ASA communications within 48 hours. Failing to do so will jeopardize your application, so please check your email and junk inbox regularly.

All applicants are required to have a nomination or a reference. If you did not receive an email saying you were nominated, please provide a reference who is within the Hopkins community (faculty, staff, alumni, current ambassador, etc.). Please confirm that the person is willing to serve as a reference before including her or his name in your application.

Please respond to each of the application prompts in 200 words or fewer. We recommend saving your responses in a text editor because the online form does not save as you go.
